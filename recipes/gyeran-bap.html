Description

Gyeran is very simple and easy to cook for breakfast. If you tired with your breakfast, you can try this dish. It spends short time to cook, saves money, and makes you healthy.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Eggs(2 eggs for one person)
  • Green onion
  • Sesame oil or butter
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients
    • Put your cooked rice to your bowl.
    • Take the green onion and thinly slice them.
  2. Take a pan and make it heat(with low heat) with your cooking oil.
  3. Crack your two eggs to the pan.
  4. Add a little bit of salt and pepper on the eggs.
  5. Don't flip your eggs and waiting until you found it enough time for your eggs.
  6. Put your eggs to your rice within the bowl. Added 1 teaspoon of sesame oil, 1 teaspoon of soy sauce, sliced green onion, and some sesame.
  7. If you don't have sesame oil and sesame, you can use butter instead.
