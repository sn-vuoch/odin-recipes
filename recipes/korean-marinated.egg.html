Description

Korean marinated egg is delicious dish popular in Korea. It also called drug egg because the flavor of it so delicious.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Red and (optional)green chili
  • Green onion
  • Sesame
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients
    • Vegetable
      • Chop the onion, red chili, green chili(optional).
      • Thinly slice the green onion.
      • Smash two cloves of garlic and chop them.
    • Sauce
      • Take a big bowl, put soy sauce, boiled water(the quantity of soy sauce and water are the same), sesame, sugar, black pepper and vinegar(optional).
      • Then mixed your sauce with prepared vegetable.
  2. Take a pot and fill it with water. Then wait until water is nearly boiling.
  3. Put your eggs to the pot and boil for 7 minutes (NOTE: If your eggs takes from fridge immediately, you must wait for your eggs about 30 minutes before boiling).
  4. While boiling eggs, gently stir them around so that the yolks can be nicely in the center.
  5. When time up! Take your eggs from pot and put them into ice water.
  6. Peel the boiled eggs.
  7. Then add peeled eggs into the sauce that we prepared.
  8. When it done! Give some time for your eggs about 6 hours or overnight in your fridge. After that, you can enjoy this dish.
