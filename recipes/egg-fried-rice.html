Description

Egg fried rice is a simple recipe that people start learn to cook at home. It's affordable but good for health. Also low-budget if you plan to save money for education like me.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Eggs(2-3 eggs for one person)
  • Green onion
  • Soy source
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients
    • Crack the eggs to a bowl, added a little of salt. Then mix your eggs.
    • Take the green onion and thinly slice them.
  2. Take a pan and make it heat with your cooking oil.
  3. Put your mixed eggs to the pan and cook for 30 seconds. Don't break it.
  4. After that, divide your egg to a half of pan then add your cooked rice to the pan. Move the egg on to your rice and mixed them for 2 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce to your egg fried rice and wait for 20 seconds,Then mixed them for 2 minutes more.
  6. Taste your egg fried rice, if it doesn't suit your taste, add some salt.
  7. After you find your suit taste, it's time to put your slice green onion to your egg fried rice to finished this dish.
